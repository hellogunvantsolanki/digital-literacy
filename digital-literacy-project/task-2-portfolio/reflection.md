Setting up my foundational digital portfolio across GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle was a critical step in establishing my professional presence. I selected these three platforms because they create a holistic digital identity for a tech student. 

Each platform serves a unique purpose: GitHub acts as my primary technical portfolio for showcasing code quality and implementation skills; LinkedIn provides the professional context for strategic networking and visibility; and Kaggle offers a specialized environment to practice machine learning algorithms and participate in data science competitions.

Over the next four years of my degree, I plan to consistently leverage and update these profiles to support my career growth. I will use GitHub to maintain a public record of my projects and open-source contributions for future internship applications. I plan to use LinkedIn to connect with industry professionals and share my learning milestones. Finally, I will use Kaggle to document my analytical approaches and build a concrete portfolio that demonstrates my competency in handling real-world data challenges.
